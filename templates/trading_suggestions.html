Trading Recommendations

Follow these guidelines to maximize your trading success

schedule

Optimal Timeframe

Our pattern recognition is optimized for a 2-hour trading window. This timeframe provides the best balance between signal clarity and execution opportunity.

balance

Risk/Reward Ratio

Maintain a minimum 1:2 risk/reward ratio for all trades. This means your potential profit should be at least twice your potential loss on each position.

shield

Always Use Stop Losses

Protect your capital by setting stop losses for every trade. Place them at logical levels based on the pattern's structure and your risk tolerance.

trending_up

Confirmation Signals

Wait for volume confirmation before entering trades. Patterns with higher confidence scores require less additional confirmation.

assessment

Position Sizing

Limit each position to 1-2% of your total portfolio. Adjust position size based on the pattern confidence score and market volatility.

show_chart

Market Conditions

Consider the broader market trend when trading pattern signals. Patterns tend to be more reliable when aligned with the overall market direction.

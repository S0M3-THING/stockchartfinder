Accuracy Metrics

Our system provides 97% accuracy in correctly identifying chart patterns. However, it's important to note that even correctly identified patterns may only lead to successful trades about 70% of the time due to various market factors. Always use proper risk management alongside our analysis.

AI Pattern Recognition

EZPZSTOCKS uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) to extract features from your uploaded charts. This powerful deep learning model has been trained to identify key visual patterns in stock price movements.

Backtested Patterns

Our system compares your chart against a database of extensively backtested stock patterns. These patterns have been rigorously analyzed for their predictive power across different market conditions and timeframes.

Pattern Matching Process

When you upload a chart, our AI extracts its key features and compares them to our pattern database using cosine similarity. This mathematical approach measures how closely your chart matches established patterns like Head and Shoulders, Cup and Handle, Double Bottom, and many more.

trending_up

Confidence Ratings

Each analysis includes a confidence rating that indicates how strongly your chart matches the identified pattern. Higher confidence scores suggest clearer pattern formations, which typically have stronger predictive value.
